## Numpad UI (Home Screen)

The main pay screen numpad is adapted from **CashApp Numpad SwiftUI** by [@txmasbo](https://github.com/txmasb/cashapp-numpad-swiftui).

- Source: https://github.com/txmasb/cashapp-numpad-swiftui
- Files: `NumpadView.swift`, `NumpadViewModel.swift`, `KeyPad.swift`
- Inspiration: [Cash App](https://cash.app)

All other screens (Activity, Money, tabs, payment flow) are original SwiftUI built for this educational clone.
